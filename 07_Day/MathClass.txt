
    Math methods are static methods and are accessed directly
    Includes PI, E for pie and the base of natural logarithms

Important Methods:

1. abs(): returns absolute value
2. ceil(): rounds up
3. floor(): rounds down
4. round(): rounds to nearest integer
5. max(): maximum of two numbers
6. mmin(): minimum of two numbers
7. pow(): calculates a raised to b
8. sqrt(): calculates square root
9. random(): generates random numbers
10. exp(), log(): exponentiation and logarithmic functions
